Introduction

The purpose of this webisite is to introduce Helen Foster Snow and her legacy to the modern world. I have included an animation visualizing her travels, a simplified timeline of her life, and hope to later finish developing an interactive map with photographs taken from the HBLL's "Helen Foster Snow Papers."

Timeline

Helen's nearly spent a decade serving the people of China - introducing Red China to the West, stoking the Student Movements, and founding the INDUSCO movement. This Timeline will highlight some of her greatest contributions and travels between the years of 1931 and 1940.
